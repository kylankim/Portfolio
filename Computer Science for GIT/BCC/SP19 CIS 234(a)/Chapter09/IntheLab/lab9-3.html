Who is World Travel?

World Travel is the leading clearinghouse and organizer for travel shows. World Travel works with travel agencies and independent agents to present a wide range of travel opportunities for all interested parties. Whether you are planning on taking your family to a quiet camping resort or for a fun-filled week aboard a world-class cruise, the World Travel Expo will introduce you to the agencies and agents who specialize in your particular needs.
