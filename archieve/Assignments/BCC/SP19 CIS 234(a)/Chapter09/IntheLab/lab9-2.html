Tao Health Food Stores

Grand Opening

Tao Health Food invites you to come join our celebration as we open our second store in the greater Boulder area. Both stores will feature specials on meats, frozen foods, healthy snacks, and fresh fruits and vegetables. We will be having a cookout (weather permitting) serving buffalo burgers, veggie burgers, and samples of grilled salmon and tuna patties.

Mission

The Tao Health Food store offers the finest and widest variety of natural, organic, and health-conscious foods. At Tao Foods, we strive to stock food that are naturally processed and use only finest natural ingredients. Our fresh fruits and vegetables are organically grown without the use of pesticides. Other foods are selected for their low fat or low carbohydrate contents. Our goal is to make hearth healthy food choices for those on restricted diets for heart disease, weight loss, or other dietary reasons.
