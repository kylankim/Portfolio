Wedding Tips

As you get closer to the big day, there will be millions of things to think about. Use this Web page a starting point for a checklist of things to do.

A Checklist Reminder

  • Soon after your engagement, plan your wedding budget and follow it.
  • Plan on a wedding Web site to keep friends and family informed.
  • Search for ceremony and reception site.
  • If necessary, identify the person who will officiate at your wedding.
  • If the reception site does not offer food, research caterers and schedule meal tastings.
  • Begin your guest list.
  • Are guests coming in from out of town? Where will they stay?
  • If going overseas, make sure passports are up-to-date.
  • Name your attendants (bridesmaids and groomsmen).
  • If attendants are from out of town, can they pick up dresses and tuxes near where they live?
  • Register for your gifts.
  • Select invitations, choose a date to mail them, and pick the date for R.S.V.P. returns.
  • Decide on live band, or DJ, then pick your music... make sure band or DJ can play choices.
  • Order flowers, cake, and reserve transportation about three to four months before wedding.
  • Plan rehearsal dinner and decide who will be invited to attend.
  • Have your gifts for your attendants to give at the rehearsal dinner.
