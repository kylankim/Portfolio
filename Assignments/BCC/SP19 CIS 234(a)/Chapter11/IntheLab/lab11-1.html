The Town of Madison Park District April Community Classes

Springtime usually means time for upgrades and repairs to homes. Whether it is just a desire to upgrade the look of a tub or shower, or fixing repairs from winter's damage, you may need some help and instruction when tackling the do-it-yourself repairs. The following classes are offered at the Town of Madison Community Center building. These classes are free, and are offered by Shillingburg Lumber and Hardware. Click the light switch on the left to see current specials at Schillingburg Lumber and Hardware.

Class sizes are limited to allow participants to have real hands-on experience.

  • Join us Saturday mornings at 10:30 to learn how to prepare a wall and apply ceramic tile.
  • Tuesday and Friday evenings at 7 p.m. learn how to replace broken light switches.
  • Need to fix a torn screen on a window or storm door? Join us Wednesdays night at 7 p.m.
  • Fix that leaky bathroom faucet on Thursday evenings at 7 p.m. and Saturday afternoons at 3 p.m.
  • All workshops are free, so please call Terrell Rayburn at 655-555-3210 to reserve space at the class of your choice.
