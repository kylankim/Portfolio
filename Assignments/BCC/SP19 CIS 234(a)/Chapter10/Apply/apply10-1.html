The Crab Apple Heights school board announces a new security and announcement program. In the event of any kind of emergency, the school district can contact you through your cell phone with an automated message. The system will also send you a text message, and finally, it will send out an email. The email option will also be used for other general announcements. Please sign up today to be on the list for notifications.

As always, Crab Apple Heights School District has the safety of your child first and foremost. We want to use this system to notify you of any changes in our daily routine that may cause you concern. Thank you for your cooperation. If you have any questions, please feel free to call the office between the hours of 7:30 a.m. and 4:30 p.m.
